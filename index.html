02 · UPL Ltd · 2025 · ✅ Shipped
LLM-powered demand forecasting & recommendation engine
OpenAI GPT · Databricks · PySpark · 12-person pod
Problem
Planning analysts were manually reviewing hundreds of SKU-level forecasts every cycle — no intelligent filter, no recommendations, no way to prioritize which forecasts needed attention. Errors went undetected until they caused stockouts and excess inventory.
My role
PM for the 12-person forecasting pod (8 data scientists, 2 data engineers, 2 visualization specialists) — defined the LLM prompting strategy, designed the analyst review interface in Figma, and built the evaluation framework for recommendation quality.
What I built
An LLM layer on top of statistical forecasting models that detects anomalies, generates plain-language root cause explanations, produces actionable recommendations with confidence scores, and routes planning exceptions automatically to the right owner.
